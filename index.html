Customer policies

Clear expectations. No confusion.

These policies help protect the customer, the property, and the schedule.

Ask a Question
Scheduling and arrival times

Service dates and times are based on availability. Arrival times may shift because of weather, traffic, previous jobs, or unexpected delays. Customers will be updated about major changes.

Weather delays

Service may be delayed for rain, storms, wet or muddy grass, extreme heat, or other unsafe conditions. Weather-delayed work will be moved to the next reasonable available time.

Quotes and pricing

Pricing depends on yard size, grass height and condition, trimming needs, access, and requested services. If the property is significantly different from the original description or pictures, any major price change will be discussed before extra work is completed.

Overgrown yards

Very tall, thick, wet, or heavily overgrown grass may cost more because it requires additional time, equipment use, cleanup, and labor.

Payment

Payment is due when service is completed unless another agreement has been made beforehand.

Property access and pets

Please make sure gates are unlocked and the work area can be safely accessed. Pets should be kept inside or safely away from the work area while equipment is being used.

Yard items and hidden hazards

Please remove toys, hoses, cords, sports equipment, decorations, large rocks, branches, trash, metal, and other items that can create a hazard or be difficult to see.

Additional work

Bush trimming, heavy weed removal, leaf cleanup, large debris removal, extra edging, and major overgrowth cleanup are not automatically included in a standard mowing price.

Photos

Before-and-after photos may be taken to document work or promote the business. Exact addresses and personal information will not be posted. Customers may ask that property photos not be used for advertising.

Customer satisfaction

If you notice an issue with completed service, contact Trent as soon as possible so the concern can be reviewed and a fair solution can be discussed.
